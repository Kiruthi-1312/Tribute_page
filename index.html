The HITMAN of INDIAN cricket


Rohit celebrating his double century

Here's an important years to remember in Rohit Sharma's life

  • 1987 - Born in Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra on 30th April.
  • 2005 - Debut for West Zone in the Deodhar Trophy at Gwalior.
  • 2007 - Made his International ODI debut against Ireland and T20 debut against England.
  • 2013 - Made his International Test Debut and Scored his First ODI double Century
  • 2014 - Scored his Second ODI Double Century and Became highest individual scorer in a single innings in ODI cricket (264 vs SL)
  • 2015 - Made his ODI World Cup Debut and Married the love of his life Ritika Sajdeh.
  • 2017 - Captained Mumbai Indains to Third IPL Victory. Scored his 3rd double century
  • 2019 - Became the third Indian batsman to win the ICC Golden Bat award at ICC ODI World Cup
  • 2020 - Became the first captain to win 5 IPL trophies.
